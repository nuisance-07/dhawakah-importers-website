# Vehicle Image Import Guidelines

When adding new vehicles and their images to the database or `public/vehicles/` directory, **always** follow this specific order for the image carousel:

1. **Cover Photo (Image 1):** The front-left or front-right angle of the vehicle, clearly showing the grille, headlights, and side profile.
2. **Second Photo (Image 2):** The hind/rear angle of the vehicle, showing the trunk, taillights, and rear bumper.
3. **Remaining Photos:** All other photos (interior, engine bay, close-ups, dashboard, side profile, etc.) can be placed in any order after the first two.

This ensures a consistent, professional viewing experience for customers browsing the inventory!
